Mechanics

Magic Mechanics

Spell Construction

Combine two or more spells to create powerful new magical effects, limitations and abilities.

Fire + Wind = Flame Tornado Water + Earth = Mudslide

Elemental Synergy

Certain elements work exceptionally well together, creating enhanced effects when combined in sequence or simultaneously.

Water then Lightning = Electrocution Earth then Fire = Lava

Mana Management

Each spell consumes mana based on its complexity and power. Efficient casting requires careful resource allocation.

Basic Spell: 10-30 MP
Combined Spell: 40-80 MP

Cooldown System

Powerful spells require time to recharge. Strategic spell rotation is key to maintaining magical effectiveness in combat.

Basic Cooldown: 2-5 seconds
Ultimate Cooldown: 30-60 seconds

NPC Mechanics

Dynamic NPC Reactions

NPCs react authentically to the player based on clothing, actions, and their own personalities, creating immersive social interactions.

A conservative priest might scold you for wearing revealing armor in temple grounds. Merchants offer better prices if you're dressed like nobility.

Adaptive Combat AI

Enemies learn from your fighting style and adjust their tactics mid-battle to counter your strategies.

Overusing fire spells? Enemies start carrying potions of fire resistance. If you rely on parrying, foes may switch to unblockable attacks.

Aggro

Each player has a dynamic threat level that influences enemy targeting. Enemies prioritize the highest-threat player, while smarter foes adjust tacticsâ€”such as using area attacks when multiple high-threat players are clustered together.

Dealing high damage increases threat. Healing allies frequently draws enemy attention.

Pack Tactics

Enemies coordinate attacks when fighting in groups, using flanking maneuvers and combined abilities to overwhelm solitary fighters.

Wolfpacks alternate between distraction attacks and pouncing from behind. Bandit archers provide covering fire while melee fighters advance.

Player Mechanics

Dynamic Fame System

Your reputation evolves based on deeds, decisions, and discoveries, opening unique opportunities as you become recognized across the world.

Slaying a legendary beast causes taverns to serve you free drinks, but also attracts bounty hunters seeking to claim your head. Repeatedly helping a faction may make their enemies hostile on sight when they recognize your insignia.

Encumbrance

Every item has realistic weight, forcing strategic choices about gear and loot. Exceeding limits impacts mobility, stamina, and combat effectiveness.

Carrying too much ore from the mines? Your dodge roll becomes a slow stumble that costs extra stamina. Opting for heavy plate armor? Expect slower spellcasting as you struggle with restricted movement.

Degrading Arsenal

Weapons gradually lose effectiveness rather than breaking suddenly, with multiple repair options available.

Battlefield repairs (quick fix): Restores 60% durability but lowers max condition Blacksmith services (proper repair): Costs gold but resets to original quality
