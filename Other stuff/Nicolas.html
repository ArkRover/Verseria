Nicolas

Knight captain

Race: Human

Personality: Arrogant

Roles: Warrior, Ambitious

Relationships

  • Nobility: Resentful of their privilege
  • Common Soldiers: Revered but sometimes feared
  • The Crown: Used for his skills but not fully trusted

Description

One of the strongest humans, a general who rose through the ranks through sheer combat prowess. His arrogance is matched only by his battlefield accomplishments.

"Medals won't win wars - I've yet to see a noble's pedigree stop a sword thrust."

Background

Nicolas came from humble beginnings but distinguished himself in countless battles. His lack of noble blood prevents him from higher positions despite his abilities.

Abilities

  • Battle Instinct: Uncanny ability to predict enemy movements
  • Iron Will: Can fight through injuries that would fell others
  • Shock Tactics: Specializes in unexpected, aggressive maneuvers

Themes

Sonne - Rammstein (Slowed)
