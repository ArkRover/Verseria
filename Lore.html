Lore

10 year flood

A third of the world experienced rain for 10 years straight. This negatively affected multiple country's especially Theilane which got the worst of it with it getting the heaviest rain between all affected countries. This led to flooding, destruction of ecosystems, food shortages and the spread of diseases. The severity of each affect where different for each country.

The great holy war

This war was a conflict started by different religions fighting each other over which is the dommonent religion.
