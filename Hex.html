Bluie

Source: Glow barries

Glow berries heal those who ingest them and has a sweet taste but eating too many especially raw can cause the Bluie disease and they are highly addictive, it is highly advised to first cook them into something like a pie before eating but this does lower the effectiveness of its healing capabilities. ...

At Stage 2: if victim continues to consume more barries more of the stomach turns into a darker blue with the same spreadding to the breasts, lips, nose and cheeks with an additional double to the previous weight gain.

At Stage 3: If the victim still consumes more barries the stomch and breasts are completely blue with it also spreading more across the face and outwards to the neck and privates, there is also noticible weight gain and breast enlargement, if the victim was thin before the transformation started they would have a roundish belly now.

At Stage 4: If the victim still continues to eat more barries they will almost completely get blue skin and get more fat.

At Stage 5: The victim is now completely blue and is double their original weight, At this point their body starts producing glowing barry juice which is a dark blue liquid, at this point if not brought a cure the victim will continue transforming without eating any more barries due to thier body producing it and it will start leaking out of the breasts and privates. These jucies are highly contagious much more than regular barry juice, if injested or even touches the skin of another person the same transformation will happen but faster in the same amount of barries.

At Stage 6: The victim is now obease continuing to get larger.

At Stage 7: The victim is immobible due to how big they are, this is their last chance to get a cure.

At Stage 8: The victim continues to expand until they pop, it is highly advised to get away from the victim before this happens because if too close a person can be completely covered in the highly contagious juice which can be enough to get to the 5th stage of transformation.
